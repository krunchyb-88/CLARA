# CLARA
CLARA (Critical Literacy and Reasoning Assistant), a health literacy tool designed to help users evaluate health and wellness claims using evidence-based frameworks.

# ⚠️ Important: Medical & AI Disclaimer
**CLARA (Critical Literacy and Reasoning Assistant) is for educational and health literacy purposes only.**

1. **Not Medical Advice:** This tool does not provide clinical advice or medical diagnoses.
2. **AI Limitations:** CLARA runs on Large Language Models (LLMs) which are known to "hallucinate" or provide inaccurate information. Never rely on an AI for health decisions.
3. **Professional Consultation Required:** Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
